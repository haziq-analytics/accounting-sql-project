# Business Questions
##Beginner

1."Can you pull a list of all customers who currently have unpaid or overdue invoices? I need their names and contact details so the collections team can start following up this week."
2."I'm putting together a vendor payment schedule. Give me a list of all bills that are still unpaid, along with the vendor name and how much we owe each one."
3."Finance wants a quick snapshot of our bank accounts — show me each account name, which bank it's with, and the current balance, sorted from highest to lowest."


##Intermediate

4."For the board meeting, I need total revenue recognized per month for this year so far. Just the month and the total — I want to see the trend."

5."One of our directors is asking how much each customer has been invoiced in total versus how much they've actually paid so far. Can you get me a comparison per customer?"
6."I want to identify our top 3 customers by total invoiced amount, so we know who to prioritize for account management calls."
7."Compliance flagged that we need to double check our books — can you verify whether every journal entry actually balances (debits equal credits)? Flag any that don't."


##Advanced

8."I'd like a proper aging report for outstanding receivables — group unpaid/overdue invoices into buckets like 0-30 days, 31-60 days, and 60+ days overdue, based on today's date. This will help us prioritize collections."
9."Management wants a rolled-up view of our Chart of Accounts — show each parent account category (like Assets, Liabilities) with the total balance of all its child accounts combined, since right now everything's siloed by individual account."
10."We're building a monthly finance dashboard. I need a report showing, for each month: total revenue, total expenses, net income, and a running cumulative net income total across the year — something we can refresh every month going forward without rebuilding the query from scratch."
